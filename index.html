About Us

At SAFELOC, we're committed to keeping you safe and informed about your surroundings.

Our web-based application is designed to provide real-time safety ratings for your current location, going beyond just reporting past crimes. We integrate various factors like crime rates and likelihood of robbery to assess safety accurately.

Real-time Safety Ratings
User-Friendly Interface
Weekly Updates

While our safety ratings are updated in real-time, we also provide weekly safety ratings to give you a broader perspective on safety trends in your area.

How We Work:

We use cutting-edge geolocation technology to determine your physical location and provide accurate safety information tailored to your surroundings. Our data integration process combines crime data from law enforcement agencies and other relevant sources to give you comprehensive safety insights.

Our Commitment:

Accuracy: We strive to provide the most accurate and reliable safety ratings to help you make informed decisions.

Scalability: As our user base grows, we are committed to adapting and expanding SAFELOC to cover new geographic locations and incorporate additional features to enhance your safety experience.
